Cup Noodle

Description

So... if you're ever broke or don't have a lot of time to cook for yourself, this dish is the way to go. The sodium content for this dish is a lot, so don't eat it too regularly for your health, but you definitely get the most bang for your buck from this delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 supermarket bought cup noodle
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Pour the water in the cup noodle
  3. Wait 3 minutes
